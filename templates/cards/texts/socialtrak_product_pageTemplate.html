Approaching this project we really wanted to rethink the common university webpage. Loads of student/faculty/staff information, news, admissions data, and events were all being displayed at once, cluttering the screen and contributing to a confusing user experience.

We decided to cut the info in to blocks, displayed the info only to those who will find it relevant. Site was converted to a responsive, intuitive design that students can access from smart phones, tables and PC.
